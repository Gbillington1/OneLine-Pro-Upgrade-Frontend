What is OneLine Pro?

OneLine Pro is a $5, one time payment, upgrade from OneLine. As of now, it comes with the Text-to-Speech feature, but Pro users will get access to every pro feature that comes out in the future.

What will I get out of OneLine Pro?

OneLine Pro allows access to the Text-to-Speech feature. This feature enables OneLine to read to you while you are using OneLine. This feature has a very positive impact, as they you now hearing the words at the same time that you are reading them. With two of your senses (hearing and sight) focused on one task, you manage to decrease the margin for reading error, leading to a more pleasant reading experience.
